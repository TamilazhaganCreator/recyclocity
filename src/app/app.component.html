Services

Bulk Waste Generators

Apartments

Waste management should begin at home , so we create awareness among the residential as our first priority because urban areas all over the country struggle with the problem of exponentially growing waste, recycle waste at the source leads to an 80% reduction in the waste that is dumped as landfills.

Tech Parks

Solid waste is a major challenge for tech parks due to the large quantity of wastes and the variability of waste characteristics and become very crucial so in order to avoid those issues our entity will assess the waste from the tech park by converting the waste into a resourceful energy and to seek waste reduction , reuse and recycling opportunities both at the individual company level and among different tenant corporate.This adoption of this method can bring both economic and environmental benefits.

Commercial Areas & Institutions

Waste generated here is quite bulk with vast types of waste and recycling is an archaic procedure in malls so our recyclocity has the best implementation plans to overcome those hurdles by creating awareness inside the commercial place and by explaining to shopping center staff and tenants to put trash and recyclables into separate containers making recycling simple and clean.
